Bio

I’m a front end web developer and recent bootcamp graduate of Thinkful’s Engineering Immersion program.

I love to learn.

I feel so lucky that I fell into the world of programming. Everyday I’m learning something new, for instance, how to handle event listeners or how to call an API with jQuery. Or I’m working on fast and efficient DOM manipulation with React. When I’m not learning something new, I’m building on previous knowledge like studying algorithm scripting or sorting through data structures. When it comes to front end engineering, the wealth of knowledge is endless. I was told that this could be overwhelming. But I honestly disagree. I find it exciting.

When I’m not obsessively going over code, I read books, mostly science fiction and fantasy. I play tennis. I play video games. I also like to cook and eat — I’m always open to recommendations for new recipes or restaurants to try.
